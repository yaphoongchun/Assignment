Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our blockchain platform and technology.

What is blockchain technology?

Blockchain is a decentralized ledger of all transactions across a network. Using this technology, participants can confirm transactions without a central authority, enhancing security and transparency.

How does blockchain enhance security?

Blockchain uses advanced cryptography and decentralized validation to ensure that data cannot be altered or tampered with. Each block of data is linked to the previous one, making unauthorized changes impossible without affecting the entire chain.

Can I use blockchain for storing documents?

Yes, blockchain can be used to securely store and verify documents. Our platform allows users to upload, share, and track documents with full transparency, ensuring authenticity and integrity.

How do transactions work on your blockchain platform?

Each transaction on our platform is securely validated by nodes in the network. Once validated, the transaction is added to a block and confirmed. This ensures the accuracy and reliability of all transactions without the need for intermediaries.
